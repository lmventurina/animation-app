How to Use the Animation Tool

1. **Draw** on the white canvas in the middle.

2. **Add a New Frame** using the large '+' button in the timeline.

3. **Onion Skin:** Check this box to see a faint outline of your previous and next drawings, which helps make your animation smooth.

4. **Play:** Use the green play button to see your animation in action. Adjust the FPS (Frames Per Second) slider to change the speed.

5. **Saving:** This tool doesn't have a file save feature. To save your work, use screen recording software (like OBS, QuickTime, or a browser extension) to record your animation playing on screen.
